# Stocks

## Stock List

Browse 80+ US equities across 10 sectors. Click any stock to view its detail page.

## Stock Detail Page

- **Price Header**: Live price with change percentage
- **Candlestick Chart**: 8 intervals (1m to Monthly) and 8 time ranges (1D to 5Y)
- **Key Statistics**: Open, high, low, previous close, volume, market cap, P/E, EPS, beta, 52W range
- **Related News**: Stock-specific news from Alpaca

Charts automatically fall back to wider time ranges when no data is available (e.g., on weekends).
